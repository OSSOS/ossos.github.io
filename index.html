The Outer Solar System Origins Survey

A 560-hour survey of the sky using MegaPrime on the 3.8-metre Canada-France-Hawaii Telescope. We aim to discover hundreds of distant icy worlds.

Welcome to the OSSOS website.

OSSOS explores the resonant populations of the Kuiper belt, surveying roughly 170 deg2 to magnitude 24.5 in r band. With dedicated tracking of our discoveries, OSSOS provides both high-quality orbits & a survey simulator for testing models of the Solar System's evolution.


OSSOS is currently in discovery phase. A catalogue of our discoveries will be released to the public via the Minor Planet Center, and publications are forthcoming. The OSSOS TNOdb page (MAKE THAT A LINK!) provides details on the detections and a correspondence table between OSSOS designations and MPC designations.


We are an international collaboration, involving 50+ scientists at institutes in Canada, France, other parts of Europe, the US, Taiwan and more. Interested in collaborating? We'd love to have more researchers on board, and you can find out how by visiting this invitation page.
