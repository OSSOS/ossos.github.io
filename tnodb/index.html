Welcome to the object database base for the OSSOS project.

Welcome to the OSSOS website.

Many visitors for this page are probably looking for the MASTER CORRESPONDENCE TABLE for the OSSOS and CFEPS project, that gives naming linkages between internal names and the Minor Planet Center designations. (For us, usage of the OSSOS/CFEPS names is useful because you know the discovery 'block' of sky coverage that the object was found in, along with its depth and search rate characterization.)
