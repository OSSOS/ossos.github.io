The MA blocks

As part of the OSSOS ensemble release, one is also able to obtain the 'MA' set of TNOs. These TNOs were discovered and tracked as part of the thesis work of Mike Alexandersen, which was peformed in a manner similar to OSSOS and CFEPS. There are two blocks, one low-latitude (with object designations malXX where XX is a number) and another high latitude (mahXX).

Some Stuff About Mike Alexandersen

He was a graduate student at UBC. He is now a postdoctoral research fellow at ASIAA.

The citation.

If you use this this object list or the survey simulator blocks, then cite: Alexandersen et al (2016) Astronomical Journal, 152, 111.
